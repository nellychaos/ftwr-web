## What FTWR Is

FTWR curates tools, documents methods, and convenes discussion about sports analytics as an intellectual field. The artifacts it publishes -- The Map, the Starters, the RFCs, the Archive, the Forum -- exist to lower the activation energy for anyone who wants to study, build, or critique models of sporting outcomes.

All content is published for **educational and research purposes**.

## No Betting Advice

Any model output, backtest result, probability estimate, expected-value calculation, or market read published by FTWR or by contributors on FTWR surfaces is illustrative. It is a demonstration of a method, not a prescription for action.

Past performance of any model, starter, or method does not predict future performance. Backtest results on historical data systematically overstate what is achievable in live markets. Markets are efficient enough that most retail participants lose money over time, regardless of the sophistication of their methods.

If you place real wagers based on anything you read on an FTWR surface, you do so entirely at your own risk and responsibility.
